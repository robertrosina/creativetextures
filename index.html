Featured Finishes

Marmorino Plaster

Marmorino is a lime- and marble-based plaster known for its natural stone look, durability, and eco-friendliness. It’s often used in high-end bathrooms and restaurants, offering a versatile and customizable surface in matte, satin, or gloss finishes.

Venetian Plaster

A luxurious wall and ceiling finish made with marble and lime. Venetian plaster is durable, toxin-free, insulating, and easy to maintain. Its layered, buffed surface gives a smooth, marble-like aesthetic that elevates both classic and modern spaces.

Feature Walls with Porter’s Paints

Transform your space with Porter’s Paints—known for their unmatched depth, sustainability, and artistic texture. Whether matte or metallic, these finishes bring statement walls to life.

Microcement

A seamless, cement-based finish ideal for minimalist designs. Applied to walls, floors, or furniture, microcement offers exceptional durability and modern elegance, suitable for bathrooms and kitchens alike.

Limewash

Limewash is a breathable, non-toxic paint with old-world charm. It adds a luminous matte texture and is great for eco-conscious designs. Though less durable, a top coat can be applied for high-traffic areas to ensure longevity and easy cleaning.

Frequently Asked Questions
  • What’s your process?
    Initial Consultation – Understanding your vision and space.
    Sample Creation – Providing bespoke samples based on your preferences.
    Application – Hand-applied with precision and care.
    Final Finish – Sealing and detailing for long-lasting beauty.
  • What’s the timeline?
    Most projects are completed in 3–5 days, depending on size and finish type.
  • How durable is Venetian plaster?
    Venetian plaster is highly durable and can last for decades with proper sealing and care.
  • Can it be used in bathrooms?
    Yes—especially finishes like Tadelakt, which are water-resistant when sealed correctly.
  • How is it different from regular paint?
    Venetian plaster is a hand-applied, multi-layered technique that offers depth, texture, and a bespoke finish.
